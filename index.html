Research Experience

I'm currently doing research on star formation in the early universe, with Dr. Chelsea Sharon at Yale-NUS College. My research is devoted to one gravitionally lensed galaxy called the Cosmic Eye, which is about 11 billion years away from us. We are trying to resolve the Carbon Monoxide line for this galaxy in order to derive interesting properties about the galaxy's star formation rate. The above plot is taken from "A very bright, highly magnified Lyman break galaxy at z = 3.07 published" in January 2007 by Smail et. al.
